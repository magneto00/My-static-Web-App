Other seafood

For more than 40 years, Marel has been helping seafood processors around the world better utilize raw materials, while also reducing processing time and increasing automation and food safety. Whatever your organizations size or goals, we have the products and services to help your business thrive. Marel is the leading global supplier of advanced standalone equipment and integrated systems to the seafood processing industry. Our innovations enable fish processors to produce better quality products in a more efficient way. From source to shelf, we have the solutions and expertise to equip the fish processing operations of today and the future.
